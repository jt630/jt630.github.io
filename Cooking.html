Salchichas Papa

Sausage and Potatoes
Any Meal
See this website

Mission Statement: When you only have potatoes and sausages. Onions are good too, but the name would be too long.

  1. Steps
    • First prep your veg. Wash Potatoes
    • Peel if desired
  2. Throw potatoes in a pan and hit em with some water. Dont cover them. Just enough water. Let it steam. Oil and season those bastards once the water is mostly gone. Onions. Peppers. Slice sausage now that your board is cleared and toss in. Garlic -- oh yeah there is garlic and peppers -- whatever you have.
    • Cook
    • Eat
  3. This is a real meal for someone before work or lunch or even dinner.   You take a starch. Cook it. Add some tough vegetables, add some cooking meat.   Optional Sear, Starch/hearty veg, hearty spices, soft veg, meat, spices (garlic), leafs (herbs)  
    • Remove bulk shit from pan
    • If lots of shit on the pan, deglaze, mustard, seasonings, butter. You can cook a countably infinite number of meals using this operation. skip all you want
